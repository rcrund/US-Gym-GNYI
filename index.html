Greater New York Invitational

The Greater New York Invitational is an annual men's and women's competition held in early December. Each year, the GNYI attracts top talent from across the country to compete in our world-class Rockland County arena. The Greater New York Invitational is hosted by U.S. Gymnastics Development Center.

Register

Register now to join us for the 13th annual Greater New York Invitational! The women's competition includes USAG levels 3 through 10, and USAIGC levels Copper, Bronze, Silver, and Gold. The men's competition includes USAG levels 3 through 10, and an elite competition.
